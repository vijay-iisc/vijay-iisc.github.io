Rasoi

Rasoi is a conceptual food design app, Rasoi aims to provide a platform where users can browse the Available Cooks, Compare, Ask for a demo and hire the Cook. This project presents an approach to solving problems faced when one comes out of his native place for a job in a new city.

Project-overview

Challange

Design a new app for working professionals where they can hire a cook for their regular meal, effectively and efficiently.

Solution

Design a new app that helps users browse available cook options, compare.

Ask for free trial and hire them for regular cooking
